Do you really LEAD?

=============

Two main responsibilities on the shoulders of a lead/leader are:

1. to create an environment for colleagues to thrive, and 

2. deliver to a consistently excellent standard.


Even a leader is a human (if not an "agent" in near future), so s/he, too, needs guidance and learning. In order to fulfill the above mentioned two responsibilities, leaders are expected to demonstrate the following traits: 

> L – Listen and be authentic,

>E – Energise and inspire,

>A – Align across the enterprise, and

>D – Develop others.
